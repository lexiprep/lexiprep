## Business Transfers (Change of Control / Acquisition)

If LexyPrep (or its operating entity) is involved in or evaluating a merger,
acquisition, investment, financing, reorganization, insolvency, or a sale or
transfer of all or part of its business or assets, your personal data — including
your account information and the content you upload together with the vocabulary
data derived from it — may be **disclosed to and transferred to the prospective or
actual successor or acquiring entity** as part of that transaction, including to
advisers during due diligence under appropriate confidentiality protections.

In that event:

- The receiving party will be required to handle your personal data under terms
  **materially consistent with this Privacy Policy**, or you will be notified and,
  where the law requires it, asked for your consent.
- We will give you **advance notice** (by email and/or a prominent in-app notice)
  before your personal data becomes subject to a materially different privacy
  policy.
- Your data-protection rights — including access, objection, and erasure — continue
  to apply, and where required by law you may object to or withdraw your data from
  the transfer.

*GDPR legal basis:* our legitimate interest in evaluating, negotiating, and
completing such a transaction (Art. 6(1)(f)), balanced against your rights and
freedoms.
